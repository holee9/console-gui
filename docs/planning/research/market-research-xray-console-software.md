### Key Observations

1. **Vendor-Neutral Leaders**: OR Technology (dicomPACS DX-R), Agfa (MUSICA), digipaX, and GoDR (JRX Diagnostics) are the only truly vendor-neutral acquisition SW solutions identified.

2. **Korean Companies Primarily Proprietary**: Most Korean DR companies (DRTECH, Rayence, Vieworks, Samsung) build console SW tied to their own detector ecosystems. DRGEM's RADMAX is a notable exception with retrofit compatibility.

4. **Cloud Gap**: Only JPI Healthcare explicitly offers cloud-based PACS/image sharing. This represents a significant market opportunity.

5. **Korean Market Strength**: Korean companies collectively produce a significant share of global flat panel detectors (Rayence, Vieworks, DRTECH) but each bundles proprietary console SW.

6. **German SW Specialists**: Germany (OR Technology, Examion, digipaX) has a concentration of independent X-ray acquisition SW vendors, suggesting this is a viable business model.

7. **Chinese Expansion**: iRay Technology and CareRay are expanding from detector-only to integrated SW solutions, potentially disrupting the low-cost segment.
